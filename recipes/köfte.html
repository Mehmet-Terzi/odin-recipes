Köfte

Köfte are meatballs from the Turkish cuisine

Classically, they are made from minced lamb and beef and seasoned with cumin.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg minced meat, beef or beef and lamb mixed
  • 2 pieces small onions
  • 2 pieces of garlic cloves
  • 1 bunch of parsley
  • 1 tsp. cumin, cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp paprika powder rose hot
  • 1 teaspoon paprika powder, sweet
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 tbsp. olive oil
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the 2 onions into very small cubes.
  2. Press the 2 cloves of garlic through a garlic press or also cut into very small cubes.
  3. Wash the parsley, shake dry and chop finely.
  4. Place the minced meat in a large bowl and add the prepared onions, garlic and parsley.
  5. Season with 1 teaspoon of cumin, 1 teaspoon each of paprika, rose hot and sweet, 1 teaspoon of salt and pepper.
  6. Mix all ingredients with your hands, incorporating 4 tbsp olive oil.
  7. Form about 12 oval meatballs from the minced meat dough and place in the refrigerator for 30 - 60 min.
  8. Fry the Köfte on the grill or in a pan (approx. 10 - 15 min).
  9. Serve with tzatziki or a dip made of yogurt, lemon, garlic, parsley and mint.
