Lahmacun

Turkish Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 kg minced meat, mixed (lamb and beef)
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 3 onions
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 bunch of parsley
  • 2 peppers, red
  • 4 hot peppers
  • 1 tablespoon margarine
  • 2 tbsp. paprika pulp
  • 4 tsp. salt
  • 3 tsp pepper
  • 100 g bacon, (lamb belly bacon)

Steps

  1. For the filling, chop all the ingredients in a food processor and mix together.
  2. Lamb belly bacon you can either directly at the butcher with the minced meat through the meat grinder or cut very small and add.
  3. For the dough, mix all ingredients together, knead into a yeast dough and let it rise in a warm place for about 30 minutes.
  4. From the dough you can form small balls and roll them out as big as a cake plate.
  5. From the dough you can form small balls and roll them out as big as a cake plate.
  6. Now you can put one or two tablespoons of the filling on each ball and spread it with a fork.
  7. Of course, the most important thing is to bake the Turkish pizza one by one in a pizza oven. Have fun with the re-baking.
